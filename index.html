    Starting out his career in nursery school as “a very tall munchkin” in a production of The Wizard of Oz, Jonathan Roxmouth would grow into one of South Africa’s most well known theatre performers. After gaining industry experience in high school with five productions at the Natal Playhouse, he jumped into a professional career and started the timeline shown here. When not winning numerous awards and captivating audiences worldwide, Jonathan can be found looking at pictures of English Bulldogs on Instagram and expanding his ever impressive collection of Funko Pop! Vinyls.
